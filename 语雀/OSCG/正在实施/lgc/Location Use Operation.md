# Location Use Operation

**Case 1.****Transfer to demo or damage location**

1、Create interal transfer

![image](_附件/1780478601295-81b225ab-e5dd-44ef-986b-68-95745905b657f313.png)

2、**Select "Internal Transfer" as the operation type, set the source location to "Stock", and the destination location to "Demo" or "Damage".**

** Under the Operations section, select the products to be delivered and enter the quantities.**![image](_附件/1780478820869-64bf0d64-9e8b-4599-aec6-75-a8a3a1a064bdefa8.png)

3、Click button “save" then click“Mark as Todo"![image](_附件/1780478979001-f8bcb255-c787-47e7-96a1-68-fcd2f4ede3e761c2.png)

4、Click "Check Availablity"  to reserve stock![image](_附件/1780479159807-dc2caa0a-8260-499f-a68c-df-f8686c89a81e3d38.png)

5、Click button "Validate" to delivery stock .![image](_附件/1780479260846-eaa64119-d2ac-4b07-9d7c-f6-4ac206e13799b9cb.png)


**Case 2.Transfer to stock to use**

1、Create interal transfer

![image](_附件/1780478601295-81b225ab-e5dd-44ef-986b-68-95745905b657f313.png)

2、**Select "Internal Transfer" as the operation type, set the source location to "Demo" or "Damage" and the destination location to "Stock".**

** Under the Operations section, select the products to be delivered and enter the quantities.**

![image](_附件/1780479810507-3da0bb22-cddd-402a-aaea-90-9e7885798fa5cd54.png)

3、Click button “save" then click“Mark as Todo"![image](_附件/1780479823098-9ab9aef0-3c2a-4d71-9c21-46-9036f3c43c2bbf07.png)

4、Click "Check Availablity"  to reserve stock![image](_附件/1780479843041-af8d5012-c426-4038-b62a-b3-fb87ff5b3ab0ebbd.png)

5、Click button "Validate" to delivery stock .Then you can use these stock.![image](_附件/1780479871356-27cb6b07-f60c-4c36-b81b-13-1ad6d72b39e148bb.png)
