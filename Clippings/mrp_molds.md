## Mold Management for MRP by SOLVVE

**Custom Odoo functionality for manufacturers of molded products**

The Mold Management for MRP introduces a dedicated mold model that combines the capabilities of both **Maintenance Equipment & Manufacturing Work Centers** - and adds mobility. Unlike standard Odoo functionality, where equipment is tied to a single machine, this module empowers molds to be used across multiple machines seamlessly.

## How does it Work?

### Redefining Mold Management for MRP

- **Flexible Mold Assignment** Assign a single mold to multiple work centers - such as blow molding machines, injection molding machines, extrusion lines, milling equipment, stamping machines, or casting units - for dynamic production workflows.

### Business Benefits

- **Realistic Planning** The system accounts for both machine availability and mold availability - enabling production planning that aligns closely with reality.
- **Increased Flexibility** No more scheduling constraints - one mold can serve multiple machines, adapting to varied production needs.
- **Enhanced Analytics** Gain deep insights into mold utilization, workloads, and production efficiency - providing groundwork for cost control, quality assurance, and wear forecasting.
![](https://apps.odoocdn.com/apps/assets/18.0/mrp_molds/image3.png?744f963)

### Advanced Capabilities

- **Maintenance Integration** Seamlessly integrates with Odoo’s Maintenance module to support scheduled repairs and preventive maintenance of molds.
- **Lifecycle Management** Track mold usage via cycle counts, monitor transfer history across machines, and forecast end-of-life timelines for proactive replacement.

### Why Choose This Module?

Standard Odoo Equipment functionality works well for generic operations - but when it comes to industries dealing with injection or blow-molded parts, standard functionality falls short. The Mold Management for MRP brings your ERP system closer to real manufacturing processes, providing accuracy, flexibility, and actionable data.
